FAQs

How do I listen to music on OMEN ?

To listen to music on OMEN:

  1. Sign up for a free or premium account.
  2. Download and install the OMEN app on your device.
  3. Open the app and search for your favorite songs or artists.
  4. Select a song or playlist to start listening.

How do I create a playlist?

To create a playlist on OMEN:

  1. Open the OMEN app and click the "New Playlist" button.
  2. Give your playlist a name and description, and choose whether to make it public or private.
  3. Search for songs or artists and drag them into your playlist.
  4. Save your playlist and start listening!

How do I follow other users on OMEN?

To follow other users on OMEN:

  1. Search for the user you want to follow.
  2. Click the "Follow" button next to their name.
  3. You can see what they're listening to by clicking the "Following" tab in the OMEN app.
