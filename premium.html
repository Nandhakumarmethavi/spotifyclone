What is OMEN Premium?

OMEN Premium is a paid subscription that gives you access to exclusive features and benefits, including:

  • Ad-free listening
  • Unlimited skips
  • Offline listening
  • High-quality audio
  • Exclusive content and experiences

How much does OMEN Premium cost?

OMEN Premium is available for ₹399 per year for individual accounts, and ₹150 per month for family accounts (up to six users). Students can also get a discounted rate of ₹50 per month.

How do I sign up for OMEN Premium?

To sign up for OMEN Premium:

  1. Go to the OMEN website or open the OMEN app.
  2. Click the "Get Premium" button.
  3. Select the plan you want and enter your payment information.
  4. Start enjoying all the benefits of OMEN Premium!

Note that you can cancel your subscription at any time, and you may be eligible for a refund if you cancel within a certain period of time.
